<b>Idaeyus</b> has never been just another software company . It never set out to be one .

From the very beginning, Idaeyus was envisioned as an open-source software collective, and that’s exactly what it remains today. But more than that, Idaeyus is a collective of  ideators .

## What Sets Idaeyus Apart?

Unlike most open-source collectives, the software we create isn’t designed for mass consumption in its original form. You might notice that it sometimes lacks certain features you’d expect from a finished product.

### Why is that?

Our goal is to empower users to customize the software we build. By leveraging AI or coding themselves , users can tailor our software to their needs , whether it’s adjusting the UI, adding new features, or reshaping it entirely. That’s why every line of code we write is open-source. 

	We ensure the base product is functional and hassle-free to use , but the true potential lies in how users make it their own.

We are not yet another software company . 
<b><i>The Software We Make</b></i> is different .
