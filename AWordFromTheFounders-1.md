
# A word from the founders 

For those who know how to code , any Idaeyus product can be modified by hand and made unique to them . But what about those who don't know how to code ?

Majority of the people in the world use AI now for accomplishing tasks . Anyone could build apps without knowing how to code  . Then why not let the users modify products that they use everyday to their liking using AI ? That's why we give out every line of code publicly - so that everyone can use it to modify the products we make .

But don't worry , irrespective of you knowing how to code , you can use any Idaeyus product as we aim to make a usable product on release , something that gets the job done . 

Also, read [The Software We Make](https://www.github.com/idaeyus/blog/blob/main/TheSoftwareWeMake.md), which is related to this article.
